Solving Quadratic Equations

Completing the Square:

It is the process of converting a quadratic polynomial or equation into a perfect square with some additional constant.

Vocabulary

Zero Product Property

The zero product property says that if the product of two numbers is 0, then one of the numbers must be 0.


Coefficient

In an algebraic expression, the coefficient of a variable is the constant the variable is multiplied by.


Constant Term

When x changes, the constant term stays the same.


Perfect Square

A perfect square is an expression that is something times itself.


Rational Number

A rational number is a fraction or the opposite of a fraction.


Irrational number

An irrational number is a number that is not rational. That is, it cannot be expressed as a positive or negative fraction, or zero.
